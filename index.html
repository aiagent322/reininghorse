What We Cover

Your Complete Guide to Reining Saddles

The reining saddle is one of the most specialized pieces of tack in the western horse world. Designed specifically to allow a rider's seat to stay deep and quiet while a horse executes demanding maneuvers — sliding stops, 360° spins, rundowns, and rollbacks — these saddles represent the pinnacle of western saddle engineering.

"A great reining saddle disappears beneath the rider — it puts you in the horse, not on it."

At ReiningSaddles.com, we've built the most comprehensive online resource dedicated to the reining saddle — from its roots in the California vaquero tradition to the modern cutting-edge designs used by NRHA futurity champions. Whether you're shopping for your first reining saddle or upgrading to a custom build, we've got you covered.
