Custom Build Process

How a Superior Saddle Is Built

01

Consultation

Andy starts every build with a direct conversation — your riding style, your horse's back, your competitive goals, and your aesthetic preferences. This isn't a form or a questionnaire; it's a real dialogue between maker and rider.

02

Tree Selection & Fitting

The right tree is selected based on your horse's measurements. Andy works with a network of trusted tree makers and can order custom bar angles for horses that don't fit standard templates. If possible, he recommends a wither tracing and back-width measurement at the 15th and 18th vertebrae.

03

Leather Selection & Tooling

Hermann Oak leather is the standard at Superior Saddles — consistent quality, proper temper, and proven durability. You'll choose your tooling pattern, finish (light, medium, or dark oil), and hardware from a full options catalog.

04

Build & Quality Check

Andy builds each saddle by hand, inspecting every stitch and every rivet. Build times typically run 8–14 weeks depending on tooling complexity and current order volume.

05

Delivery & Follow-Up

Your saddle ships fully assembled and ready to ride. Andy follows up after the first 30 days to ensure fit and comfort — and he stands behind his work if any adjustments are needed.
