The Smart Buy

Why Certified Used Makes Sense

💰 Real Value, Premium Quality

A custom reining saddle from a top maker holds its value extraordinarily well. A certified used saddle from a reputable source often gives you $4,000–$7,000 worth of quality for $1,500–$3,000 — fully inspected and guaranteed.

🔍 Leather That's Already Broken In

A quality saddle that's been properly used and conditioned is often better than a brand-new one — the leather is pliable, the tree has settled, and the seat has shaped to the natural riding position. Broken-in leather is a feature, not a drawback.

🏆 Access to Top Makers

Custom saddles from premier makers often have wait lists of 6–18 months. The used market lets you ride a Bob's Custom Saddle, Circle Y show saddle, or other premium maker's work immediately — without the wait.

✅ David's Guarantee

Every saddle in David Solum's certified inventory is personally inspected for tree integrity, rigging soundness, leather condition, and hardware function. David stands behind what he sells — period.

The Certification Standard

What "David Solum Certified" Means

🌳

Tree Check

Tree integrity verified — no twists, breaks, or structural issues. Flex test performed.

🔩

Rigging Inspection

All rigging hardware checked for cracks, wear, and proper function. Replaced if needed.

🛠️

Leather Condition

All leather surfaces assessed and documented. Cracking, stretching, and repairs noted.

📏

Measurements

Seat size, gullet width, skirt length, and tree width measured and verified accurate.

📋

Full Report

Complete condition report provided with every certified saddle — no hidden surprises.
