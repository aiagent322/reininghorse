This AM Superior Saddlery build pairs their signature #1 flower tooling with a classic Nevada border — the decorative rope-twist border that frames the skirt and jockey panels. At 16½ inches, this fits a wide range of riders and is an ideal choice for anyone who wants a large-seat Superior build in a dark, classic color palette.
